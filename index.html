March 25th - March 26th, 2017

What is Hack.UVA?

A programming event that will be held on Saturday, March 25, 2017 to Sunday, March 26th, 2017 where you, along with other developers, designers, and entrepreneurs work together to create a web or mobile application/platform from scratch. Prizes will be awarded to the top hacks. There is no entrance fee and all meals are free.

Who can attend?

Hack.UVA welcomes all current students. If you are an organization interested in sponsoring the event, please contact hackathon@virginia.edu.

The judges will evaluate your hack based off creativity, polish, and technical vigor. Succeed in all three categories, and you're bound to win!
