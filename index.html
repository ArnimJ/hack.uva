The Fourth Annual Hack.UVA Spring 2017

What is Hack.UVA?

A programming event that will be held Spring 2017 where you, along with other developers, designers, and entrepreneurs work together to create a web or mobile application/platform from scratch. Prizes will be awarded to the top hacks. There is no entrance fee and all meals are free. Look forward to more information coming soon!

Who can attend?

Hack.UVA welcomes all current students. If you are an organization interested in sponsoring the event, please contact hackathon@virginia.edu.

When can I register?

Registration will open in the spring!

How big can my team be?

Between 3 and 7 feet tall. But seriously, you may work in a team with up to four people.

How does judging work?

The judges will evaluate your hack based off creativity, polish, and technical vigor. Succeed in all three categories, and you're bound to win!
